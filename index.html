Red Velvet

A girl k-pop group that debuted in 2014 with 4 members(Irene,Wendy,Seulgi,Joy) later on in 2015 another member was added(Yeri). They are one of the most popular group in the newer generations of k-pop. Each one of these members take on a role of being the leader,main vocalist, lead vocalist, rapper, and dancer. They just had a comeback a couple weeks ago with their album called Peekaboo.


Irene

Leader / Main Rapper / Lead Dancer

Irene is the leader of the group and is the oldest in the group. She was born on March 29, 1991 in Daegu, South Korea. Her real name is Bae Joo Hyun and her stage name is Irene. Even though she is the oldest she can sometimes act as if she is not with her members. She can also sometimes be shy.

Seulgi

Lead Vocalist / Main Dancer

Seulgi's full name is Kang Seulgi and she is in charge of Dancing and is a lead Vocalist. She was born on February 10, 1994 in Ansan, South Korea. Fans and her members say that she has no jams. Which means she can sometimes be boring, but still she shines on her own.

Wendy

Main Vocalist

Wendy is the member who is in charge of speaking English since she studied/lived in the U.S. and Canada. Her real name is Son Seung Wan and she was born on February 21, 1994 in Seongbuk-dong, Seoul, South Korea. And she is in charge of being the Main Vocalist.

Joy

Sub Vocalist / Lead Rapper

Park Soo Young aka Joy is basically a bundle of joy. She was once the maknae(youngest) in the group but now her role has been filled by someone else. She was born on September 3, 1996 in Jeju Island, South Korea.

Yeri

Maknae (Youngest) / Sub Vocalist / Rapper

Kim Ye Rim aka Yeri is the youngest in the group. She is a Sub Vocalist and a Rapper in the group. She was added in 2015 during their ICC(Ice Cream Cake) era and has been in the group since then.
